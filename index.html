Editing your personal information in sApp is simple and straightforward:

  1. Log in to your sApp dashboard
  2. Click on your profile picture in the top-right corner
  3. Select "Account Settings" from the dropdown menu
  4. Navigate to the "Personal Information" tab
  5. Update your details (name, email, phone, etc.)
  6. Click "Save Changes" to update your information

All changes are saved instantly and will be reflected across the platform.
